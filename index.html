About Us


An Underground House Music Record Label, Which Aims To Unearth Talented House Music Artists/Producers & DJs' Whether From South Africa Or Other Countries Around The Globe & Establish Its Brand On The House Music Scene.

The Label Was Started In 2022 By South African DJ & Producer Jonathan Peters (DJ Jonnas)

Since Its' Inception The Label Has Signed These Artists To Its' Roster:

Adam Jesse-Harmony Mills, Aquarius, DJ Jonnas, Endjour, FrankNoMore, K-Elements, KRU-SIDE, SizBlack, Teddy M...&... We Are Still Searching For More Artists' Which Have Yet To Be Discovered


Sub Genres Include: Amapiano, Afro, Deep, Electro, Electronica, Funky, Jackin, Minimal, Nu-Disco, Progressive, Soulful, Tech, Tropical, Tribal, Techno & More...
